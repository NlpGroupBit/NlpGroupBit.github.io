ABOUT US

Data tells stories! At Data Intelligence REsearch and Cognition Technology (DIRECT), we believe that every dataset holds insights waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to explain raw data (including text, image, video and unstructured data) with natural language and transform into actionable intelligence through cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, cognitive computing, and complex-problem reasoning.

Our team comprises researchers and practitioners committed to interdisciplinary collaboration, pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve. At DIRECT, we not only harness the power of data but also ensure that our solutions are ethical, transparent, and impactful. Join us in shaping a future where data truly tells the story—one that drives progress and empowers humanity.
